  • About ME

    • MY NAME IS IEK CHIMBWANDA

    I'm a passionate Director and Screenwriter based in South Africa, driven by a love for learning and my desire to create visually captivating films that showcase the beauty and storytelling potential of Africa.

    Africa is a continent that never fails to inspire me with its breathtaking landscapes, diverse cultures, and most of all, its untold stories.

    Through my films, I aim to capture the essence of Africa and bring its hidden narratives to light, connecting with audiences on a deep and emotional level.

    Film is a powerful tool that has the ability to transport us to different worlds, evoke emotions, and tell compelling stories. I believe in utilizing this power and am committed to using it to its fullest potential.

    As a continent, we have tons of stories to tell, from folktales to real events that took place over the years that deserve to be seen by the world.

    My goal is to bring some of these stories to life in a way that captivates a worldly audience and leaves a lasting impact.
